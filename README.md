# HR Analytics Absenteeism Dashboard

## Short Description / Purpose
The HR Analytics: Employee Absenteeism Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze employee absenteeism patterns within an organization. The dashboard provides insights into absence hours, health conditions, seasonal trends, and reasons for absenteeism, helping HR teams understand workforce attendance behavior and identify factors contributing to employee absence.

This project enables organizations to monitor absenteeism trends and support data-driven HR decision making to improve workforce productivity and employee well-being.

## Tech Stack
The dashboard was built using the following tools and technologies:

- Power BI Desktop – Main platform used to build the interactive dashboard
- Power Query – Used for data cleaning and transformation
- DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) – Used to create calculated measures such as average absenteeism hours

## Data Source
The dataset consists of HR absenteeism data containing employee attendance information and related attributes.

Main datasets used in the project:

- Absenteeism_at_work.csv – Employee absenteeism records
- Reasons.csv – Reasons for employee absence
- compensation.csv – Employee compensation information

The data includes factors such as:

- Absence hours
- Health condition (Healthy, Overweight, Obese)
- Transportation expenses
- Day of week and seasonal trends
- Reasons for absenteeism

## Features / Highlights

### Business Problem
Employee absenteeism can significantly impact productivity and operational efficiency. HR teams often struggle to identify patterns in absenteeism and understand the underlying causes behind frequent employee absences.

Without proper analysis, organizations cannot effectively develop strategies to reduce absenteeism and improve workforce engagement.

### Goal of the Dashboard
The objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor employee absenteeism patterns
- Identify key health and behavioral factors affecting absence
- Analyze seasonal and weekly absenteeism trends
- Understand primary reasons behind employee absence
- Support HR decision making for workforce management

### Walkthrough of Key Visuals

#### Key HR Metrics
The dashboard highlights important absenteeism metrics including:

- Average Absenteeism Hours: 6.92 hours
- Total Employees Analyzed: 740
- Total Absence Hours: 5,124 hours

These KPIs provide a quick overview of workforce absenteeism levels.

#### Health Condition Analysis
A set of donut charts compares absenteeism across employee health conditions:

- Healthy employees contributed 58.37% of total absenteeism hours
- Overweight and obese categories accounted for a significant share of absence hours

This helps HR teams understand the relationship between employee health and absenteeism.

#### Monthly Absenteeism Trend
A line chart shows average absenteeism hours by month, highlighting seasonal variations and periods with higher absence rates.

#### Weekly Absenteeism Pattern
Another line chart analyzes absence patterns across days of the week, helping identify which days experience the highest absenteeism.

#### Absence Reasons Analysis
A bar chart displays the most common reasons for employee absence, including:

- Medical consultation
- Unjustified absence
- Symptoms and medical conditions
- Physiotherapy
- Follow-up medical visits

This helps HR departments address the most frequent absence causes.

#### Transportation Expense Analysis
A scatter plot visualizes the relationship between transportation expense and average work load, helping identify potential factors influencing absenteeism.

## Business Impact & Insights

- Identified average absenteeism of 6.92 hours per employee, providing a benchmark for workforce attendance.
- Revealed that healthy employees accounted for 58.37% of total absence hours, highlighting that absenteeism is not limited to health-risk groups.
- Helped HR teams identify key absence reasons such as medical consultations and unjustified absence.
- Provided insights into monthly and weekly absenteeism patterns, supporting better workforce planning.
- Enabled data-driven HR strategies to reduce absenteeism and improve employee productivity.
